Technology as a Tool

Year 2022
Category Video, Filming, Editing
Tools Camera, Premiere Pro

Technology as a tool — a journey through the experiences of two visual communication teachers at SUPSI, focusing on the technologies that accompany their work. The interviews were conducted with two teachers of different ages and experience levels to highlight the differences in when technology was introduced into their practice and how it has evolved.
